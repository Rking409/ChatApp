ChatApp

Encrypted real-time messaging for night owls. Chat, share moments, and stay connected with end-to-end encryption.

Features
End-to-End Encrypted
Messages and photos are encrypted in transit and at rest. Only you and the recipient hold the keys.
Real-time Chat
Instant messaging with WebSocket-based delivery. No refresh needed — messages arrive as they're sent.
Daily Moments
Capture and share photos with your rooms. Moments unlock at night — a daily ritual for you and your friends.
Dark by Default
Built for the night. A deep, dark interface with amber accents that's easy on the eyes in low light.
12 Languages
Fully localized interface including RTL support. Chat in your language with friends around the world.
QR Code Sign-In
Seamlessly transfer your session from mobile to desktop by scanning a QR code. No passwords needed.
How to Install
1
Open ChatApp in your browser Visit the app URL on your mobile device. Sign in with Google or create an account.
2
Android — Install the PWA Open Chrome, tap the menu (⋮) → "Add to Home Screen" → "Install". An icon will appear on your home screen just like a native app.
2
iPhone / iPad — Install the PWA Open Safari, tap the Share icon → "Add to Home Screen" → "Add". ChatApp launches in full-screen, just like a native app.
3
Done — you're all set! The app works offline, receives updates automatically, and feels just like a native app. No App Store needed.
